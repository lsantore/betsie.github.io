Our Story

The Betsie app is a product of Squeaky Cheese, Inc., founded by veterans of the tech and games industries, Kat Strafford and Laetitia Santore. After years of leading product development at major tech and entertainment companies, they recognized the need for a modern, safe, and fun way for friends to make predictions and friendly wagers on things they care about, and not for money.
