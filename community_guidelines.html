Betsie – Community Guidelines

These guidelines help ensure Betsie is a safe, respectful place for adults to have fun with friendly predictions.

1. Adults Only

Betsie is for users 18+. No content involving minors is allowed, including bets about:

  • Actions of minors
  • Outcomes involving minors
  • Sensitive personal info about minors

2. Keep It Safe and Friendly

Bets are meant to be light-hearted. Do not create bets that involve:

  • Coercion
  • Pressure
  • Humiliation or targeted harassment

Participation must always be voluntary.

3. No Gambling

Bets on Betsie must never involve:

  • Money
  • Gift cards
  • Items of real-world monetary value
  • Anything that could be sold or traded

All stakes must be symbolic or social.

4. No Harmful or Dangerous Bets

Prohibited topics include:

  • Self-harm
  • Excessive drinking or drug use
  • Physical harm or stunts
  • Encouraging illegal activity

5. Sensitive Topics Are Not Allowed

Do not create bets about:

  • Someone’s health
  • Sexual activity
  • Personal trauma
  • Criminal accusations
  • Protected personal attributes

6. Respect Others

Prohibited behaviors include:

  • Bullying or shaming
  • Threats
  • Insults or harassment
  • “Gang-up” bets used to target a person
  • Posting someone else’s private information

7. Allowed Advertising

Ads will appear in the app. Do not:

  • Harass advertisers
  • Try to manipulate ad systems
  • Post content that violates ad network rules (including hateful or explicit content)

8. Reporting Issues

Report harmful content through in-app tools. We will take action as appropriate.
