Our Story

Betsie was founded by industry veterans Kat Strafford and Laetitia Santore through their partnership, Squeaky Cheese, LLC. After years of leading product development at major tech and entertainment companies, they recognized the need for a modern, safe, and fun way for friends to make predictions and friendly wagers on things they care about, and not for money.

We believe that predictions are a core part of human connection. Betsie is designed to be the dedicated space for those fun, low-stakes agreements that deepen friendships and add a competitive spark to everyday life.
